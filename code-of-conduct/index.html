Code of conduct

http4s is a Typelevel incubator project. We hope that our community will be respectful, helpful, and kind. We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of gender or anything to do with it (identity, history, expression, non-binary gender, etc.), sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion, and programming background.

People are expected to follow the Typelevel Code of Conduct when discussing http4s on GitHub, Gitter, and other venues provided by the http4s project.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact a member of the community staff, such as:

  • Ross A. Baker
